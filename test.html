Star Viz Amazon Turk Survey Results - revised 7/70

Analysis by Adam Black awblack@mmc.org

Thursday, July 30, 2015

This page presents the results from the third round of Amazon Turk survey using the updated star visualization. The data were downloaded from the google speadsheet at https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1RteQ2fRMcJj_4VVvhM4SvRecWuCg55pAxKBNPOtM9io/edit?usp=sharing on 7/1/2015. The survey used to generate this data can be found at http://people.ucsc.edu/~jamcohen/interactiveViz/vizTemplateLocal_Stars.html.

Participant counts: 299 Turkers were randomized to three viz types; Star viz, Text Only, and Star viz + text. Two participants were excluded for incorrectly answering the sanity check question.
